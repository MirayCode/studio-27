The Brand

TONI&GUY is a multi-award-winning global hairdressing brand with more than 50 years of experience in education, superior client service and haircare expertise. TONI&GUY hairdressers are educated beyond industry standards and trained to deliver the most creative and precise hairdressing in the world.

What we will offer you:
  • A work visa to enable you to relocate
  • Free access to all of our Toni&Guy Digital Academy weekly training.
  • Service & Retail commission : huge earning potential
  • Medical Health care insurance provided FOC
  • Round trip ticket to home country
  • 4 weeks paid holiday per year
  • Regular, free haircut and colour
  • Unrivalled career development opportunities worldwide, including Salon Management and Franchise Partner roles, and opportunities at all TONI&GUY worldwide locations.
Stylist and Technician role:
  • Full consultation with clients.
  • Building your client base and retention rates, ensuring that all clients receive the authentic TONI&GUY experience
  • Referring clients for colour and increasing client product knowledge and product sales.
  • Acting as a role model in the salon and acting as an ambassador for our brand.
  • Playing a key part in representing and promoting the TONI&GUY brand and super brand status at all times.
  • Something something
